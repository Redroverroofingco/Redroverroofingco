# Red Rover Roofing

**Family-owned residential roofing contractor in Spring Hill, Tennessee.**

Red Rover Roofing is a veteran-owned, woman-owned roofing company specializing in premium roof replacement, storm damage restoration, and insurance claim advocacy across Middle Tennessee. Founded in 2025 by Alex Hostetler and Courtney Hostetler, a retired U.S. Army Captain.

**Legal Entity:** Red Rover Construction LLC (d/b/a Red Rover Roofing)

## Service Area

Red Rover Roofing serves four counties in Middle Tennessee:

- **Williamson County** (primary) — Franklin, Brentwood, Thompson's Station, Nolensville, Spring Hill
- **Davidson County** — Nashville metro area
- **Maury County** — Columbia, Spring Hill
- **Rutherford County** — Murfreesboro

---

## Services

- Roof replacement (full tear-off and re-roof)
- Storm damage inspection, repair, and restoration
- Insurance claim assistance with Xactimate-formatted documentation
- Emergency roof tarping
- Gutter installation and repair

### Premium Roofing Systems

- CertainTeed Landmark, Landmark PRO, Grand Manor, Presidential Shake TL
- Class 4 impact-resistant shingles (UL 2218)
- Brava Roof Tile synthetic slate
- Standing seam metal roofing (steel and aluminum)
- Copper ridge caps, flashing, accent panels, and copper gutter systems
