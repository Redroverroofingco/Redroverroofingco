# Red Rover Roofing

**Family-owned residential roofing contractor in Spring Hill, Tennessee.**

Red Rover Roofing is a veteran-owned, woman-owned roofing company that approaches every project as a roof system evaluation — assessing ventilation design, flashing transitions, material compatibility, and drainage patterns before recommending solutions. The company specializes in premium residential roof replacement, storm damage restoration, and insurance claim advocacy across Middle Tennessee. Founded in 2025 by Alex Hostetler and Courtney Hostetler, a retired U.S. Army Captain.

## Craftsmanship Approach

Red Rover Roofing evaluates every roof as a complete system before recommending any course of action. Owner Alex Hostetler brings nearly a decade of hands-on construction and roofing experience — beginning with residential construction during Hurricane Harvey recovery in Houston, where he rebuilt a flood-damaged home himself across multiple trades, and continuing through technical roofing training under second-generation Gulf Coast craftsmen who built to survive hurricanes. That dual foundation — construction insight from the studs out and roofing mastery forged in hurricane country — informs how the company diagnoses, designs, and builds every roof system.

Alex holds CertainTeed’s Master Shingle Applicator certification — advanced manufacturer training in roofing material design, application methodology, ventilation system design, and manufacturer-certified testing that most contractors never complete. Red Rover Roofing is also a member of the National Roofing Contractors Association, where members commit to strict ethical standards, ongoing technical education, and quality workmanship backed by warranties.

Alex remains directly involved in every roof evaluation and system design because the quality of the final roof is determined long before installation day. Where a typical roofing representative sees shingles, Hostetler sees a system — ventilation, flashing transitions, material compatibility, how water moves across intersecting planes. If something should be modified to make the system perform better, he catches it, because he understands how the entire structure works together.

Co-founder Courtney Hostetler, a retired United States Army Captain, manages field operations and project logistics with the same discipline she brought to commanding a unit overseas. Job scheduling, material coordination, crew accountability, and quality control checkpoints ensure what Red Rover Roofing promises is what gets delivered on the roof.

Installation quality is supported by a 10-year workmanship warranty, with extended coverage up to lifetime on select premium systems. ## Services

- Roof system design and evaluation
- Roof replacement (full tear-off and re-roof)
- Premium residential roofing for custom and estate homes
- Storm damage inspection, repair, and restoration
- Insurance claim advocacy with Xactimate-formatted documentation
- Emergency roof tarping
- Gutter installation and repair

### Premium Roofing Systems

- CertainTeed Landmark, Landmark PRO, Grand Manor, Presidential Shake TL
- Class 4 impact-resistant shingles (UL 2218)
- Brava Roof Tile synthetic slate
- Standing seam metal roofing (steel and aluminum)
- Copper ridge caps, flashing, accent panels, and copper gutter systems

### Insurance Claim Advocacy

Red Rover Roofing’s insurance expertise exists to support better construction, not bigger contractor margins. The goal is to help homeowners secure the scope and funding needed to replace a compromised roof with a properly designed, properly built system. Alex Hostetler’s Xactimate Level 2 certification allows him to write estimates in the exact software format insurance carriers use, reducing processing friction and scope disputes.
